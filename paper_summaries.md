# Summaries

### Assigned Readings

#### A . Property rules, liability rules, and inalienability: one view of the cathedral. Calabresi, G., & Melamed, A. D

‘One view of the cathedral’ is one of the most famous and widely-cited  legal theory articles of all time. Its analysis of how legal  entitlements can be categorised provides an analytical basis for  understanding how intellectual property is structured, and how the  concept liability rules can be used to contrast with exclusive rights.  This idea of ‘property rights vs liability rules’ is a theme that will  come up consistently during your learning in intellectual property law.
