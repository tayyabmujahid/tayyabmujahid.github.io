## Notes

**Delaney Dataset**

- Regression dataset
- Molecular structures and water solubility of 1128 compounds
- Molecular structures are encoded in SMILES string
- SMILES is way of writing 3-d chemical structures in a computer software readable format
- This a popular dataset to validate ML models on the task of predicting the solubility of a compound based on its molecular structure
